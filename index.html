Crystal Collector!

Can you get the exact number?

A random number will be generated at the beginning of the game.

The four crystals below will have a random value assigned to them that will be hidden from you until you click on it.

Win the game by matching your total score to the random number. If your score goes over you will lose and the game will reset.

Each time the game starts over the values of the crystals will change.
